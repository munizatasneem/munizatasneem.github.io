Featured research

Peer-reviewed, published, and making a case for why every word in the courtroom carries weight.

● Published · March 2025

A Critical Discourse Analysis of Language Used in Courtroom Cross-Examinations in Pakistani Dramas

Journal Liberal Journal of Language & Literature Review
Category Y-Category (HEC Recognized)
Co-author Iram Rubab
Framework Fairclough's 3D Model of CDA

This study examines the linguistic choices legal participants use to assert dominance during cross-examinations in Pakistani television dramas. Drawing on Norman Fairclough's three-dimensional framework of Critical Discourse Analysis, it unpacks how syntax, modality, and turn-taking become instruments of power — inside the courtroom and on screen.
